Baba Ganoush

Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 2 medium eggplants
  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Prick the eggplants with a fork and place them on a baking sheet. Roast for about 30-40 minutes, turning occasionally, until the skin is charred and the flesh is soft.
  3. Remove the eggplants from the oven and let them cool. Once cooled, peel off the skin and scoop out the flesh into a bowl.
  4. Add tahini, olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, and salt to the eggplant flesh. Mash everything together until smooth.
  5. Transfer the baba ganoush to a serving dish and garnish with fresh parsley. Drizzle with a little extra olive oil if desired.
  6. Serve with pita bread or fresh vegetables.
